Breakfast Recipe: Avocado Toast

This simple and delicious avocado toast recipe is perfect for a healthy breakfast. Just spread ripe avocado on toasted whole grain bread, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and add your favorite toppings like a poached egg, cherry tomatoes, or feta cheese. Lunch Recipe: Quinoa Salad

For a nutritious and satisfying lunch, try this quinoa salad. Mix cooked quinoa with diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onions, and parsley. Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Add crumbled feta cheese and chopped almonds for extra flavor.

Dinner Recipe: Grilled Salmon

This grilled salmon recipe is easy and delicious. Marinate salmon fillets in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs. Grill over medium-high heat for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork. Serve with steamed vegetables and rice.
